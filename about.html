About Me

Film Editor & Aspiring Data Scientist

I’m Corinne. I’m based in Northern California and my educational background at Chapman University and my career so far have been focused on filmmaking, specifically editing and post-production.

Prior to my Data Science Master’s program with Bellevue University, I started out learning Python online and at Hackbright Academy. I’ve been particularly intrigued by the overlap between artistic fields and data science, and I’m always looking for more opportunities to be creative with data science.

I’ve been passionate and immersed in film since the first grade, so it has been quite a switch delving into the world of data science. Integrating data science in film post-production is something I’m looking forward to exploring in more depth.

Outside of film-related endeavors, I spend my time traveling, reading, baking, creating art, and seeking out quality time with family, friends, and animals.
